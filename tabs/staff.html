Leadership Descriptions

Camp Half Blood

  • Oracle - Alexa
  • Gives out prophecies to campers and sends them on quests.
  • Camp Director - Mac
  • Oversees everything at Camp Half Blood and has the final say on big decisions.
  • Cabin Counselor - See bottom of page
  • Oversee all the activity within their cabin, help improve the camp, and make big decisions.

Camp Jupiter

  • Praetor - Cal & Nat
  • The two(2) elected leaders of camp Jupiter. They act as leaders in the all of the legion's affairs, including battles.
  • Centurion - See bottom of page
  • There are two(2) centurions per cohort, and they oversee all the activity within their cohort and act as senators.

Hotel Valhalla

  • Captain - Lucy
  • One of the many captains of the Einherjar. As Captain of the Valkyries, she is their leader, and ranks just under Odin in their hierarchy. As captain of the lower floors of Valhalla, she commands part of the army of Valhalla. She is in charge of floors 2-25, and she commands every soldier "living" on those floors. The only ones who rank higher than Captain are the Thanes, the personal guards of Odin when he goes into battle, but those are NPCs only.
  • Valkyrie - See bottom of page
  • Choose slain heroes to bring to Valhalla and become einherjar.

House of Life

  • Pharaoh - Tiffany
  • The head of the House of Life.
  • Chief Lector -
  • The head magician of the House of Life. Positioned in the First nome, this title is held by the most powerful magician in the world, who are adorned with a leopard skin cloak.
  • Godling - See bottom of page
  • More experienced magicians who host a god. They don't have an official rank in the House of Life hierarchy but they are respected and listened to.

The Hunt

  • Lieutenant of the Hunt - Arcadia
  • She is in charge of the Hunt. She watches over the other hunters and possesses the tiara given to her by Artemis. The tiara marks her post and enhances her connection to Artemis (enhancements include: A small increase in power. And a way to mentally talk to Artemis at any time and way). The Lieutenant also administers the binding oath.

Job Descriptions

Infirm Jobs

  • Head of the Infirm -
  • Takes care of many jobs behind the scenes and takes care of patients also, they select new people to become medics, therapists, and nurses.
  • Medic
  • Work in the infirmary, taking care of people from when they walk into the waiting room, until discharge.
  • Nurse
  • Assist the medics in taking care of patients, and mainly work in the overnight zone.
  • Therapist
  • Help characters to process their trauma and move forward with their lives.

Forge Jobs

  • Forge Master -
  • Takes care of many things behind the scenes while also working to fulfill orders, they select new forge staff and decide when each member of their staff levels up.
  • Forge Worker
  • Fulfill orders that are not enchanted and make the items to be enchanted to fulfill other orders.
  • Enchanters
  • Use their magical abilities to enchant items made by the forge workers in order to fulfill orders.
