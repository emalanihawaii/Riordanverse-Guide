Creating Your Character

These are some things to help you while building your character. There are a few things that differ from faction to faction. This will help you make sure your bio is correct for the faction that you want to join.

Greek

To start off in the Greek Faction your character:

  1. Must have somehow gotten to CHB
  2. Get claimed in their backstory or RP being claimed

Roman

To start off in the Roman Faction your character:

  1. Start their demigod journey in Wolf House
  2. Before getting a tattoo the character needs either a year of service OR prove themselves with an act of valor

Norse

To start off in the Norse Faction your character:

  1. Die with a weapon in hand defending someone
  2. Get claimed by a Valkyrie and be taken to Valhalla

Egyptian

To start off in the Egyptian Faction your character:

  1. Have Pharaoh blood of some sort listed in their bio
  2. Not attract monsters
  3. Include all future powers in the backstory

Hunter

To join the hunt your character:

  1. Be a female maiden
  2. Take the hunter's oath
  3. Have no romantic attachments

To join in bio please include:

  1. How the character found the hunters
  2. Why the character joined the hunt
  3. Personal conversation with Artemis
  4. If the character was rescued by any current hunt members
