All godly paths can also learn how to use the duat (duat travel, duat sight, duat lockers, etc.), however it takes more practice to learn.All godly path can make shabti and charms however it is very hard to do properly.All godly paths can learn how to do combat avatars but it’s by far the hardest out of the four (unless specified on your list).

With practice you can control what it looks like, where you go, and when it is trigger. This can be overridden though by a person more powerful than you and can have consequences.
