Demigod/Legacy Abilities

Dagda

General Ability - Insight
Children of Dagda have increased wisdom, knowledge, and quick thinking, due to their godly parent being the god of Wisdom
Pick 3:

  • Light of Life
  • Children of Dagda can sense the life forces of others. This does not include the sense of someone’s imminent death; rather, it allows a child of Dagda to sense the strength of a life force or the weakness of it. They are also able to know mortals from demigods or others without seeing the person.
  • Aura Reader
  • Children of Dagda are able to see the auras of others. It manifests in different ways depending on the child, such as being able to tell someone’s mood from their aura, or understand intentions based on auras. Some are even able to tell the emotions of someone based on their aura alone.
  • Emotional Manipulation
  • Children of Dagda have the ability to create emotions in others or force other emotions on someone. They can also intensify current emotions that someone is feeling to an overwhelming level, or use emotions to sway people into doing what they want.
  • Voice of Reason
  • Children of Dagda can make weaker-minded people see the logic in their side. This ability is not as strong as charmspeak, and more easily shaken off, but can still be used as a form of magical persuasion.
  • Spellcaster
  • Children of Dagda have spellcasting abilities related to wisdom, energy, earth, and plants. This includes spells related to draining someone mentally, making them sleep, and anything with herbs or plants. They are unable to perform any actual spells or enchantments, however.
  • Siphon
  • Children of Dagda have access to a form of magic that does not need any ingredients or help. They can simply drain someone of their energy to the point of near-sleep or at the very least passing out. The energy goes to them, and, unlike any other spell, both parties are affected. This can also be done in reverse where they can give someone else their energy as well, but this is very hard to do and requires lots of training.
  • Fruitful
  • Children of Dagda have the ability to increase fertility around plants, but are unable to grow plants or control them. They can also revive plants completely from death should the plant die. This ability is strongest in fruit plants and trees

Morrigan

General Ability - Warrior
Children of Morrigan have increased combat abilities and swordsmanship skills. They also innately know swordsmanship basics with or without prior experience with weapons
Pick 3:

  • Corvid Call
  • Children of Morrigan are able to speak to crows and ravens via spoken tongue, as both birds are related to death and prophecy. They will also be recognized and loved by these birds as children of Morrigan regardless of claiming status.
  • Prophecy
  • Children of Morrigan can sense outcomes and have foresight into choosing the correct path. Minor visions of the future that will not be clear to them. They also can sense when something isn’t right or when bad things are about to happen.
  • Black Night
  • Children of Morrigan have the ability to tell when someone is dying or not in good health. When death is near for the intended person the death aura will intensify until they are healed or pass on.
  • Tangled Threads
  • Children of Morrigan are able to 'read' the destinies or fates of others. They can tell when someone has a tragic destiny or fates, as well as heroic destiny if this is written for them. This differs from prophecy because it is only aligned with individuals, not situation - moreover, nothing can be done to change the outcome.
  • Energy Grant
  • Children of Morrigan have the ability to lend others energy. Unlike siphoning energy, this is a one way exchange and can only be given. Energy can be given to one individual only to assist them in powers or possibly stall fatal injury. This is extremely draining and can be very dangerous for both members, and requires training to use.
  • Strength in Numbers
  • Children of Morrigan can help increase the powers and energy of a large group of demigods. They cannot create powers and are only able to call upon this power in times of great need. The energy will increase the general durability and abilities of the entire group.
  • Telumkinesis
  • Children of Morrigan have natural proficiency with common weapons (spear, shield, sword, bow, etc, but not chakrams, whips, etc). They are generally more skilled with these weapons than other demigods with the same training.

Lugh

General Ability - First Glory
Children of Lugh are natural leaders and have high intuition regarding strategic insight. They also have a high sense of morals and justice as well as a strong moral code, and are often seen as the 'righteous' person of the friend group due to this. Their aura is also very strong, and often draws people in to them whether consciously or unconsciously
Pick 3:

  • Bolt of Vengeance
  • Children of Lugh are able to curse people in vengeance and force them to exact vengeance on someone who has wronged either the target or the caster - once vengeance has been exacted, the curse will cease to have power over the target. This works similarly to the abilities of the children of Nemesis.
  • True Instincts
  • Children of Lugh can tell when someone is lying. They also have heightened awareness and perception around others, which makes it easier for them to call bluffs, sense lies, and overturn deceptions.
  • Voice of Command
  • Children of Lugh can impose their will on weaker-minded people for a maximum time of 5 minutes. This ability functions as a weaker edition of charmspeak and similarly makes the user slightly more resistant (but not immune) to charmspeak and other mind attacks.
  • Disarm
  • Children of Lugh are able to defuse tense situations by spoken word. This includes high intuition towards strong emotions and strongly relies on strength and willpower of the speaker.
  • Lightning Strike
  • Children of Lugh have the ability to manipulate electricity and lightning. With practice, they can control this lightning and direct it towards their targets. A child of Lugh is naturally resistant to electricity damage, but their power will manifest as a discharge of electricity that damages all around them if they are untrained. They are also able to concentrate these abilities in certain areas of their body, and some have mastered it enough to be able to charge a phone simply by holding the charger.
  • Trodaire
  • Children of Lugh are advanced warriors with enhanced combat skills and weapon-related abilities, especially the spear. Most children of Lugh prefer a spear or a ranged weapon to a sword, but they are equally skilled with all three should the situation call for it.

Danu

General Ability - Deep Blue
Children of Danu are able to sense water and communicate with aquatic animals via spoken language, as well as be recognized by the aquatic animals as a child of Danu regardless of claiming status. They are also resistant to fire damage and burning in general, and prefer to be near water when possible
Pick 3:

  • Depthsurge
  • Children of Danu can manipulate existing water to suit their needs. This includes shields, ropes, walls, and other uses. The greater the creation of water, the harder it is to manipulate and the more energy it takes. They are only able to manipulate water around them in a close radius; it also takes training for a child of Danu to be able to accurately control the water to their will.
  • Heart of Water
  • Children of Danu are able to breathe underwater just as easily as if they were on land. Their lungs are unaffected by water so long as there is adequate oxygen in the water, although it takes training to be able to shift from breathing air to oxygen. Most children of Danu are only able to sustain this ability for short periods of time.
  • Rushing Waters
  • Children of Danu have the ability to heal themselves or others using water to heal wounds. Short of any imminent life-threatening injuries, they can heal most anything with enough water, time, and energy.
  • Minor Thermoregulation
  • Children of Danu are able to control the temperature around them to a small degree. They can change the weather to become colder or warmer as they will it, but are unable to summon storms, high winds, or snow.
  • Dark Tide
  • Children of Danu are able to bless or curse water, or bodies of water, at their disposal. The larger the body of water, the harder it is to bless or curse, and the more energy it takes. Curses or blessings include but are not limited to healing blessings, clearing dirty water, spoiling water, turning it undrinkable, etc.
  • Water's Blessings
  • Children of Danu can draw strength from bodies of water or water in general. Once submerged in water, their abilities slightly increase and they tire less often. This also works in reverse, as a child of Danu deprived from water will see their powers weakening instead.

Cernunnos

General Ability - Untamed
Children of Cernunnos are able to communicate with all animals via spoken language. They will also be recognized by all animals regardless of claiming or demigod knowledge status. They also have a natural sense of direction and can always tell where the cardinal directions are
Pick 3:

  • Overgrowth
  • Children of Cernunnos are able to control existing plants to their needs. While they are unable to grow plants or revive them, they can manipulate trees, vines, branches, etc, in order to suit their needs. They can also communicate to plant spirits and the like via spoken language, and will be recognized as children of Cernunnos by plant spirits.
  • Fierce Core
  • Children of Cernunnos can draw power and energy from nature. Just being in an open, grassy space, or a forest, energizes them greatly and feels like a full night of sleep to them, for example. They can also drain power out of plants and animals in nature (too much drainage will kill the animal/plant so children of Cernunnos tend to be careful with how much they pull).
  • Feral
  • Children of Cernunnos have the ability to tame wild animals or vice versa via spoken words. They also have heightened intuition regarding wild animals and are able to command animals to do their bidding to a degree with this ability. This ability also allows them to track wild animals and enhances their tracking abilities regarding animals in general.
  • Call of the Wild
  • Children of Cernunnos possess a panic-causing cry that amplifies the fear in everyone who hears it. With practice, this cry will only affect enemies - or those seen as enemies. Extended usage of this power will damage the user's vocal cords.
  • Chaos Ruler
  • Children of Cernunnos are able to draw power from wild and chaotic things. They experience an increase in strength and energy when placed into a chaotic situation, and are able to increase their powers from the fear of others to a degree.
  • Deadly Nightshade
  • Children of Cernunnos are immune to any plant-based poison or poisonous plants, such as poison ivy or trumpet vine. They also have an extended knowledge of such plants and more often than not are able to identify them based on sight alone

Brigid

General Ability - Poet's Eloquence
Children of Brigid are natural poets regardless of formal instruction or lack thereof. They are also able to understand most languages regardless of training and can speak fluently in many different languages as well
Pick 3:

  • Healing Rays
  • Children of Brigid are able to heal others and themselves using rays of light. Usually, they can heal most major or minor injuries, but even their abilities are limited - children of Brigid cannot reattach limbs or heal nerves in the case of broken spines.
  • Light of Dawn
  • Children of Brigid are able to manipulate light and intensify the heat of light as well. They can also brighten light and summon it, even in complete darkness. The light they use is as good as sunlight in their hands and functions as such.
  • Entangle
  • Children of Brigid have the ability to facilitate plant growth, specifically the growth of flowers. This ability works on all plants, but it is most effective when dealing with the growth of flowers.
  • Rising Sun
  • Children of Brigid become more powerful as the dawn approaches, drawing strength from dawn and experiencing an increase in their energy and ability level at this time. They are most powerful right at dawn, and least powerful at dusk. Most children of Brigid are usually nocturnal and prefer the night to the day if possible, because of this.
  • Glowing Aura
  • Children of Brigid have a brighter aura than most, regardless of claiming status. It's possible to physically manifest this power and glow like a lightbulb with enough practice, but most children of Brigid only have a spiritual aura of light, while others glow faintly in the dark instead.
  • Haze
  • Children of Brigid can create illusions and turn objects 'invisible' by bending light, either around an object or to create the appearance of an entirely new one. This works on objects about the same size or smaller than a person only.

Aengus

General Ability - Timeless
Children of Aengus appear youthful for longer than other demigods. They are also more attractive to other demigods and radiate a captivating aura to others regardless of claiming status
Pick 3:

  • Musicality
  • Children of Aengus have music-like charmspeak, which enables them to use a type of hypnotism or persuasion in which it allows the speaker to convince someone else to do or get whatever they want. The strength of the command depends on the tone and the emotion of the charmspeaker's voice, as well as their skill with it.
  • Prince Charming
  • Children of Aengus can sense attractions to themselves or others and minorly influence attractions. They are unable to create attraction to someone, however, and can only influence what is already there.
  • Lovey Dovey
  • Children of Aengus are able to create minor love charms and curse others relating to love. This only works for a short amount of time, and curses can be but are not limited to be cursing someone's love life, forcing someone to love another, etc, etc (but they must be used with consent OOC).
  • Bardic Inspiration
  • Children of Aengus are able to play most instruments regardless of formal training. They can also play instruments to hypnotize others and put people into trances via music, which works similar to charmspeak and essentially makes the person a quasi-puppet for a short amount of time (must be used with consent OOC).
  • A Thousand Voices
  • Children of Aengus are able to affect emotions in others. They cannot create emotions, nor can they remove emotions, but they are able to heighten or lower emotions in others. They can also project their own emotions over others to a degree, but can never do it fully.
  • Potion Making
  • Children of Aengus have the ability to create potions to suit their needs. This includes healing potions, damage-dealing splash potions, etc, but poisons are not covered in this list.

Ceridwen

General Ability - Grimoire
Children of Ceridwen are naturally talented with herbs and are able to identify herbs based on touch alone. They are also skilled with medicinal plants regardless of previous experience
Pick 3:

  • Witch's Familiar
  • Children of Ceridwen may summon one animal to aid them in their spellcasting, magic works, and day to day life. All familiars can communicate to their owner via mind link and speak to other animals of the same breed (However, the owner can only understand their familiar). The familiar enhances the owner, but not vice versa (animals can’t pick up on their owner’s powers and are unable to use them).
  • Enchantress
  • Children of Ceridwen are able to use magic via spoken spells or intention. These spells are mostly minor abilities that work in the moment - i.e. magic projectiles, shields, etc (check with an Admin for larger spells).
  • Master of Potions
  • Children of Ceridwen have the ability to create potions to suit their needs. This includes healing potions, damage-dealing splash potions, etc, but poisons are not covered in this list. These potions work on both people and animals as well as the user.
  • Mists of the Cauldron
  • Children of Ceridwen can manipulate the Mist into illusions, shields, or other such things. They are also able to alter their appearances or the appearances of others temporarily with this ability. Continued use drains energy exponentially.
  • Perception Check
  • Children of Ceridwen are able to identify curses, traps, and enchantments on objects. With enough practice, they can also remove curses, traps, and enchantments on objects, or enchant objects temporarily.
  • Shape-Change
  • Children of Ceridwen can shapeshift into animals. This ability is draining the larger the animal is, and it does not always guarantee a shift back. Treat this as a poly-morph, in which you take on the animal's mind as well as shape.

Arawn

General Ability - Spirit-Sight
Children of Arawn can identify the auras of those about to die, or see the spirits of those recently dead in the place that they died. They can also sense deaths that have happened to those nearby, or their friends
Pick 3:

  • Night-Walker
  • Children of Arawn can use the shadows as a form of movement, stepping through the shadows to move from any location to another location so long as there are shadows for them to use. This ability takes practice to use and drains a user exponentially should they use it consistently over a short period of time.
  • Darkness Blessed
  • Children of Arawn draw strength from the night, becoming more powerful in the darkness and shadows. They are at the height of their power on nights of the new moon, where their every power is maximized to its full potential should they need it, but in the day, they are slightly weaker.
  • Hell-Raiser
  • Children of Arawn have control over necromancy to a minor degree, enabling them to see and raise spirits and speak to ghosts as well as re-animate anything smaller than they are. The larger a re-animation, the more energy it takes.
  • Touch of Shadow
  • Children of Arawn are able to control and manipulate the shadows to a small degree. With practice, this ability can be used to create shields, heal others, solidify the shadows into tangible objects or weapons, and other such uses, but the more things are made, the harder it is to control all of them.
  • Wild Hunt
  • Children of Arawn are granted a hellhound familiar to aid them in battle and in their day to day life. This hound has enhanced tracking abilities and are seen as a guiding presence; they are also extremely loyal and hard to kill, but once killed, take a while to regenerate, like any other monster.
  • Vengeance
  • Children of Arawn can reflect attacks, curses, and magic back on enemies - the larger the power/attack, the harder it is to reflect. If it's too big to reflect back safely, the power/attack will then be turned aside and rendered useless for a short time.

Cailleach

General Ability - Frostproof
Children of Cailleach are resistant to cold and extreme temperatures, although heat is harder to resist. They are also less likely to be affected by extreme situations, including but not limited to extreme heat, cold, lack of oxygen, electricity, etc
Pick 3:

  • Touch of Frost
  • Children of Cailleach are able to manipulate snow, wind, and rain. They are able to freeze objects and summon constructs of ice, wind, or snow; the larger the area of effect or object, the harder it is to maintain the frozen state.
  • Snowstorm
  • Children of Cailleach have the ability to summon storms. This includes but is not limited to rain, snow, sleet, hail, etc. Conversely, they can also lessen or increase the effect of storms provided it is not hurricane level, but this is a little bit harder and lot more draining.
  • Gale Force
  • Children of Cailleach have a minor control over winds. They can manipulate, control, and shape the air and winds to a small degree, and on some occasions, turn it tangible for short periods of time
  • Winter is Coming
  • Children of Cailleach can control the temperature and lower it to simulate feelings of winter. This includes chill, snow, wind, etc; the larger the area affected, the harder it is to control and keep steady, and the more energy it drains.
  • Frostbite
  • Children of Cailleach can cause plants in their near vicinity to wilt. This ability can be increased or decreased at will - as a passive ability, plants nearby will begin to freeze, taking on a droopy appearance, and prolonged exposure can kill plants entirely.
  • Unforgiving
  • Children of Cailleach can drain energy from one person, animal, or plant at a time (not fatal, it’s really slow and augments you only). This is a slow process, and can only be applied to the user.

Neit

General Ability - Impassioned
Children of Neit are much stronger and faster than regular demigods. They also excel in strategies of war and are naturally talented with strategizing and thinking ahead
Pick 3:

  • Enchanter
  • Children of Neit can enchant or curse weapons and armor, or any type of battle-gear. Able to see curses or enchants on any object, but can only remove or add curses/enchants on weapons and battle gear. Able to place a mark on weapons to summon them (up to 5).
  • Fearmonger
  • Children of Neit are generally very intimidating to others and radiate a minor aura of fear. This ability can be "turned off", but it can also be increased to a level of heart-stopping fear for a few moments at maximum depending on the user's preference. The more intense the fear, the greater the energy drain. Can discern people's fears by being in close proximity with them.
  • Natural Combatant
  • Children of Neit have the ability to pick up any weapon and use it - however, this does not mean use it well nor master it - but the ability will do in a pinch. They are naturally talented in all types of combat regardless of previous training.
  • Raging Fury
  • Children of Neit are able to manipulate emotions of war, such as anger and hatred. This includes enhancing them, creating them, or removing them entirely. With enough practice, they can do this to larger groups of people, but the larger the group, the harder it is.
  • War Cry
  • Children of Neit are able to easily scream and yell long distances and even short-term deafen someone by screaming at them. Easily heard when speaking, typically has a deep voice or a deeper register than others around them. With practice, this can be concentrated into a shout loud enough to shatter glass, but overuse of this power strains vocal cords quickly.
  • True Shot
  • Children of Neit are naturally skilled with ranged weapons and projectiles. Their thrown projectiles naturally curve towards targets within reason, and their aim is almost supernatural, able to make shots that may not be accessible for mortals.

Áine

General Ability - Daybreak
Children of Aine are stronger in the daytime, and at the height of their power at noon when the sun is at its peak. They are slightly weaker in the nighttime from the absence of the sun
Pick 3:

  • Voice of Persuasion
  • Children of Aine can make weaker-minded people see the logic in their side. This ability is not as strong as charmspeak, and more easily shaken off, but can still be used as a form of magical persuasion.
  • Sovereign Protection
  • Children of Aine are resistant to fire, and immune to sunburns and heated temperatures. They also have a higher durability against magical attacks, and are generally luckier than most demigods in games of chance or similar scenarios.
  • Flashpoint
  • Children of Aine have the ability to manipulate light, namely to create flashbangs. With practice, this ability can be used to create shields, heal others, solidify the shadows into tangible objects or weapons, and other such uses, but the more things are made, the harder it is to control all of the
  • Summertime
  • Children of Aine can change the temperature and environment around them to mimic a summer setting. This includes heat, warmth, sunlight, etc; the larger the area affected, the harder it is to control and keep steady, and the more energy it drains.
  • Natural Leader
  • Children of Aine are natural leaders who attract other leaders as well. People feel like they should listen to children of Aine and they are naturally persuasive and charismatic as well. They are also able to see the results of certain choices made in battle.
  • Shape-Changer
  • Children of Aine can change their shape into that of an animal for short periods of time. The larger the animal, the harder to change into; treat this as a polymorph in that you slowly adapt traits of that animal the longer you're in wild form.
