Leadership

  • Lieutenant of the Hunt - Avarelle
  • She is in charge of the Hunt. She watches over the other hunters and possesses the tiara given to her by Artemis. The tiara marks her post and enhances her connection to Artemis (enhancements include: A small increase in power. And a way to mentally talk to Artemis at any time). The Lieutenant also administers the binding oath.
