Mist Manipulation

Who Can Manipulate the Mist

  • Children of:
  • The Big Three
  • Trivia
  • Mythical Creatures

What You Can Do With the Mist

  • Summon Mistforms
  • Charmspeak
  • Teleportation
  • Create Powerful Illusions
  • Manipulate Memories(minor)
  • Hide the Real Forms of Things from Humans and Demigods
  • Hide Locations, People, Beings, and Objects
  • Disguise People and Beings

Rules for Manipulating the Mist

  1. You must be trained or be in a position of power
  2. You must ask permission from the admins(ooc) before you start to learn mist manipulation(ic)
  3. If you abuse your power of mist manipulation, there will be consequences
