Creating Your Character

These are some things to help you while building your character. There are a few things that differ from faction to faction. This will help you make sure your bio is correct for the faction that you want to join.


To start off in the Norse Faction your character:

  1. Die with a weapon in hand defending someone
  2. Get claimed by a Valkyrie and be taken to Valhalla

Floor placement

The floor assignments for those who enter Hotel Valhalla go by the age the character was when they died. Each floor holds a range of ages. Once you find the floor check in there before you RP. this will be your living area at Valhalla. Thanks for spending your afterlife with us.

  • Floors 10-13: Age 13
  • Floors 14-17: Ages 14-17
  • Floors 18-20: Ages 18-20
  • Floors 21-24: Ages 21-24

This is subject to change as we grow.
