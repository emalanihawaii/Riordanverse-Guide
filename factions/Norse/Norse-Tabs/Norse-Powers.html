Odin

General Ability - Perception Check
Good at looking past physical or obvious things, able to see the layers of magic on items including curses or blessings (but not identify them). Able to see when or where powers have been used (but not what powers) and can identify magic items hiding in plain sight (like the nine ducks portal to Yggdrasil).
Pick 1:

  • Seeker of Truth:
  • This includes advanced wisdom, knowledge, and a love for learning. In addition, users also have the ability to detect lies of any kind (omission, twisting of truth, etc) from any living being, and can also compel targets to tell the truth via spoken word. This truth does not need to be something consciously remembered, and the pressure to speak truth can only be resisted by extreme force of will. The user is also able to see through illusions, although it’s harder to see through the Mist than regular illusions…think trying to open your eyes to bright glare.
  • Divine Wisdom:
  • Read the behaviors of others and deduce accordingly. With enough practice, this enables the user to become somewhat like Sherlock Holmes, being able to read between the lines and see what’s not there in order to solve problems. Those with this ability are often able to read people easily, including feelings of others, and should they choose it, they will be able to manipulate others easier than other demigods. They are also able to read any language (read only) regardless of whether or not it is spoken in the present day.
  • Shapeshifter:
  • Able to shapeshift into any humanoid form, living or dead, provided they have seen an image or have an idea of what they want to shift into. This shape cannot be held indefinitely and slowly drains the energy of the wielder over time (max time, 12 hours - once time is up, you just revert back to normal form very drained). One must be in natural form to shift into another form - you can’t shift from one form to another form.

Pick 2:

  • Warp:
  • Have the ability to create minor illusions that cannot be seen through except by other children of Odin. These illusions range from creating illusions of living things to changing the perception of reality, but they will only last for 5 minutes unless deactivated beforehand.
  • Loyal Attendant:
  • Able to summon up to three animals and compel them to become the user’s assistants in any situation. Can speak to any animal via spoken language and understand them. Cannot control any animal, but can minorly influence those not in the compelled three. This works best on ravens and wolves, but other animals will do in a pinch.
  • Polearm User:
  • Extremely experienced with staff or other similar spear-like weapons regardless of previous experience or learning. Hard to miss when throwing javelins or other ranged weapons that have been learned (that are not polearms).
  • Rhythmical Composition:
  • Skilled in poetry, rhyming words, writing, etc (i.e. very good in rap battles). This also includes minor skill at lying - enough to fool a mechanical lie detector, but not a human one. However, most use this ability rarely as they dislike lies and falsehoods.

Frigg

General Ability - Farsight
Can see in 4K vision - see small details or things that may be overlooked by others. Pays attention to details and external flaws, able to see much farther than a regular demigod (such as seeing the eyes on flying birds overhead, seeing the individual hairs on a lion’s tail, etc).
Pick 1:

  • Clairvoyance:
  • Can see into the future or past using divination tools like cards, crystal balls, trances, etc. They are often able to accurately predict things close to the present time (up to 1 week in the future, RP time), but accuracy decreases as time goes on. Unable to predict major history-altering future events due to being blocked from interference with major events.
  • Supreme Guidance:
  • Extremely persuasive naturally, but can also influence people to make a ‘right choice’ by spoken word (think like charmspeak). Almost impossible to resist, especially if the choice specified is in alignment with the ‘good’ side of the moral compass (resistance comes from strength of will).
  • Personal Flurry:
  • Have the ability to create personal clouds for a short time (5-15 minutes, 15 minutes max). These clouds can do anything from providing shade, creating and providing rain and calling vicious storms (but no lightning). Can only create up to 10 medium sized 20-inch clouds at one setting. Requires an hour cooldown after the 15 minutes is up due to the strain it puts on the body.

Pick 2:

  • High-Functionality:
  • Usually smarter/wiser than most demigods, able to multitask easily and think quicker as well as have faster reflexes. This includes skills of deduction, observation, and other skills. Able to understand all human languages regardless of whether or not they are spoken, but unable to actually speak all human languages without hearing it at least once.
  • All-Mother:
  • Naturally appear friendly or welcoming to others regardless of personality. Animals and people alike are drawn to them. They can communicate with all animals and summon them for aid, but cannot force them to obey the communications via spoken language. Can also communicate with plants, but cannot summon or command them.
  • Jaeger:
  • Unconstrained by any boundaries, possesses an extremely accurate internal compass, can track anyone provided they have a close belonging or possession of that person. Able to remove minor curses from objects or people. Partially immune to sickness or disease, but not entirely immune.
  • Weaver:
  • Good at textile crafts like knitting, crochet, weaving, embroidery, etc. Good at manipulating threads or ropes as a baseline. Able to work at textile crafts extremely quickly regardless of having potential experience. Generally called towards textile crafts if given a choice.

Thor

General Ability - Combative
Easy to anger and quick to fight, but also able to use general handheld weapons although usage of an unknown weapon will always be worse than someone who has practiced with it, and it will never be mastery - just a general idea of how to use it…which is just pick it up and swing it.
Pick 1:

  • Stormbreaker:
  • Can summon storms, lightning, and thunder. This includes but is not limited to rain, snow, sleet, hail, etc. Conversely, they can also lessen or increase the effect of storms provided it is not hurricane level, but this is a little bit harder and more draining, and can only be used once a day.
  • Electrokinesis:
  • Able to manipulate electricity and lightning, and can imbue it in their weapons to increase damage dealt, in addition to using it to speed up their reflexes and movements in battle. Resistant to electricity shocks and other lightning abilities, but not completely immune (i.e. being hit with a taser would do nothing, but getting struck by lightning feels like getting hit with a bucket of Legos).
  • Super-Strength:
  • Much stronger than the average demigod, able to pick up various giant things with ease regardless of their own personal size. This is an ability that can be ‘turned on/turned off’, unlike their father’s super strength, so they can give people high-fives without breaking their entire arms, but they can also pick up full file cabinets without dropping anything.

Pick 2:

  • Intimidation Check:
  • Generally very intimidating to others and radiates a minor aura of fear. Good at being persuasive, can bluff people somewhat easily, decent at diplomacy, just overall very charismatic.
  • Goat-Kinesis:
  • Able to speak to, summon, and charm any and all goats. Can communicate via spoken language and are usually recognized by goats as children of the deity regardless of claiming status. With extreme concentration, they can see through the eyes of a nearby goat and essentially 'possess' it for 5 minutes (maximum time).
  • Hammer Time:
  • Skilled with hammers and axes regardless of experience. Hard to miss if using throwing axes or just flat out throwing their hammer/axe (but this only applies to hammers and axes…they’ll definitely miss throwing a dagger unless they’re otherwise trained). Can summon said hammer or axe (one at a time, no double-summon) provided it is in a nearby 5-foot radius. Also, for some reason, they’re good at dancing.
  • Dolby Stereo:
  • Extremely loud. Able to shout and yell long distances and even short-term deafen someone by screaming at them. Easily heard when speaking, can yell loud enough to shatter glass for brief periods.
  • Potting Soil:
  • Plants grow quicker in their presence. They cannot intentionally change un-fertile soil into fertile soil, but existing plants will grow quicker and plants planted by them will yield more fruit or flowers than others of its type.

Sif

General Ability - Giantess
Stronger than most average demigods, able to pick up various giant things with ease regardless of their own personal size. This is an ability that cannot be ‘turned on/turned off’, so children of this deity must be very careful with their strength so as not to injure others. Most children of this deity learn it early and subconsciously do so after some time.
Pick 1:

  • Geokinesis:
  • Able to manipulate the ground around them by touch or gesture. Can use the earth as an extension of themselves with seismic sense and feel things around them. However, they cannot manipulate steel or metal - it must be untainted earth. Can create walls, shields, weapons, etc, out of the ground, but the bigger it is, the harder it is to make. Shields would be easier to make versus a storm of asteroids, for example.
  • Blood of the Earth:
  • Control, manipulate, and create lava and/or heat. Able to raise temperatures and endure higher heat levels than other demigods. Will not burn when touching heated objects, and is able to control existing lava in addition to creating it. Too much lava, however, will often force the user into overheating (think a high grade fever).
  • Hair-Kinesis:
  • Can cut off their hair and weave it into a tangible, solid object, with the exception of money or monetary change. In addition, their hair requires special scissors to cut, which can only be used by a child of the deity. Their hair grows faster than most demigods (usually about six inches a day, with a total cap length of down to said child’s ankles) and often requires unconventional manners to deal with it, but each strand of hair is like fine wire that is hard to break. Those with this power also have skill in crafts and arts pertaining to yarn.

Pick 2:

  • Miracle-Gro:
  • Have the ability to control plants and speed up their growth, or make them grow in previously unfertile soil. Existing plants grow faster in their presence, and plants planted by them will grow faster and yield more product than others of its type.
  • Restoration:
  • In addition to general knowledge of healing herbs, plants, and potion combinations, children of this deity are able to heal others with special mud and plants they create. This works best for topical surface injuries, but with enough practice, this works for major, although not life threatening injuries as well.
  • Harvester:
  • Drain energy from one person, animal, or plant at a time (not fatal, it’s really slow and augments you only). Users are also skilled at using a scythe or sickle in combat. This also works in reverse, where one can augment someone else’s energy over a slow rate by taking it from animals, plants, or others (not yourself).
  • Guardian:
  • Skilled in combat and martial arts. Generally proficient with a sword-shield combo, but all other weapons are just as good. While they are not able to immediately master weapons without some training, they can use any weapon rudimentarily for about 15-20 minutes.
  • Family Ties:
  • Able to see the godly parentage of any demigod regardless of claiming status and prior knowledge of the god. If it’s a god that is unknown or wishes to be undiscovered, the parent will appear as a shadowy, invisible figure to the child of the deity.

Ran

General Ability - Collector's Edition
Able to touch any tangible object and detect enchantments, curses, blessings, and damage to it, as well as detect the date, age, and material it was made from. This extends to all mythical and non-mythical objects. Also generally very tricky and good at making bargains.
Pick 1:

  • Hydrokinesis:
  • Can manipulate, control, and summon water. Most times, the water summoned will be salt water, and they control salt water best, but this also works with other forms of water. However, this ability does not extend to liquids - only water.
  • Stormterror:
  • Create thunderstorms and gale-strength winds to destroy enemies. These storms last for about 10 minutes before slowly fading away, and are naturally stronger when created by the ocean or a strong body of water.
  • Drowned Specter:
  • Summon up to five of the spirits of the drowned to fight on your behalf or otherwise fulfill biddings of the summoner. Able to talk to the spirits of the drowned regardless of summoning them or not, but they must be near the body of water in which the person drowned.

Pick 2:

  • Shipwrecker:
  • Channel the destructive ability of the ocean, increasing your physical and demigod abilities (including powers) to near-cataclysmic levels. This enhancement can apply to others if touched, but works best applied to onself, and wears off after 30 minutes or until manually deactivated.
  • Undercurrent:
  • Draw strength from nearby bodies of water - the larger, the better. Stepping into water physically speeds up their healing abilities and strengthens their physical and mythical abilities as well. Some are also able to heal others with nearby standing water, but can only heal minor wounds during this time (nothing bigger than the palm of your hand).
  • Call of the Sea:
  • Can speak with all aquatic animals via spoken language and read their thoughts. Also able to see through their eyes and hear what they hear for a limited time (5 minutes, any longer would physically overload the senses). Will be recognized by aquatic creatures as the child of this deity regardless of claiming status.
  • Snare:
  • Proficient with rope, string, knots, and other such crafts, as well as the usage of a fishing net as an offensive weapon. Can use their net to trap and disarm/disable enemies and attackers. Nets in their possession are usually much stronger than other fishing nets.

Loki

General Ability - Jotun’s Strength
Stronger than most average demigods, but not as strong as children of Thor, for example. Able to ‘turn off’ and ‘turn on’ the ability when necessary. Able to resist higher extremes of heat and cold than other demigods, as well as have higher durability and stamina than other demigods.
Pick 1:

  • Shapeshifter:
  • Have the ability to shift into any animal or person smaller than yourself (no elephants if you’re not 10 feet tall, for example). Shifts are limited to five shifts per day - this ability uses up a lot of energy to shift and shift back, so only five shifts back and forth are allowed per day. Each new form counts as one shift.
  • Pyrokinesis:
  • Mastery over and manipulation of existing fire, as well as the creation of flame from your own body’s energy (you can’t burn down a forest - the flame involved would kill you). Can also control heat.
  • Honeyed Words:
  • In addition to being a good liar, able to fool mechanical and general human lie detectors (unless it’s an ability), the child is able to use a type of hypnotism or persuasion in which it allows the speaker to convince someone else to do or get whatever they want.
  • Weapons of War:
  • Can change anything into a weapon in a pinch, but they can’t do it often. The weapons they create are ‘Made’ into something more dangerous than what they are before, and they never break or wear down. Users can only ‘Make’ a weapon once every few days due to the drain it takes on their magic.

Pick 2:

  • Serpent’s Tongue:
  • Able to speak with snakes and reptiles, but mainly snakes - via spoken language. Can summon, charm, and speak with snakes, as well as be recognized by snakes (only if claimed). Extremely convincing in general language if they wish to, in addition to being convincing when speaking to snakes.
  • Illusory Touch:
  • Have the ability to create illusions (one at a time) to be viewed by all as a certain illusion, as well as touch someone and create illusions that only they can see (although this one is a harder feat to perform). Illusions will dispel if touched, or if dispelled by the caster. They naturally dispel after about 15 minutes.
  • Devourer:
  • Able to eat anything (including poisoned food, inedible things like shoelaces, etc) without any negative physical repercussions (like indigestion), given that the starting thing was edible in the first place. No eating bone, metal, ‘dead’ things so to speak.
  • Assassin’s Blade:
  • Skilled with bladed weapons such as swords, daggers, knives, etc. Innate skill regardless of being trained or not, but they are unable to master it without formal training. Most children of this deity also gravitate towards bladed weapons as opposed to bludgeon weapons.

Freyr

General Ability - Junior Seafarer
Have minor nautical skills, including a vague internal compass (can tell which direction's north, but can't tell anything else) and a decent sense of direction. Skilled at fishing and nautical rope abilities. More resistant to sheer cold and can hold their breath for longer.
Pick 1:

  • Peace of Frey:
  • Expel energy in a 10-foot radius shockwave and disarm all traps, render all weapons (magical and non-magical) unusable for the next 15 minutes, and create a peaceful aura to all in the immediate vicinity (if you can see the user, you'll get the aura).
  • Summertime:
  • Temporarily change the area around the user (10-foot radius) into a summer-like setting; this includes elevating the temperature and lightening the area. It only lasts for about an hour before deactivation (manually or due to time limits), but during this time, temperature and light can increase dramatically from heat/light manipulation.
  • Derecho:
  • Summon rain clouds and create a temporary windstorm and/or thunderstorm (for 15-20 minutes). Once deactivated, the clouds dissipate and leave sunshine in its place regardless of the previously occurring weather.

Pick 2:

  • Alf Seidr:
  • Have the ability to heal either himself or others from wounds and injuries. This also comes with a glow that sustains from the wound while it heals as well as for a short time after. Larger injuries take more time and energy to heal and depletes more stamina than smaller injuries.
  • Germination:
  • Can control and manipulate plants, including making them grow faster, or causing plants to grow where they normally cannot regardless of season. Plants under their control can break through rocks and restrain people for short amounts of time.
  • Telumkinesis:
  • Skilled with sword-like weapons and similar weapons; able to pick up any sword and use it well, and pick up any other weapon with a vague idea of how to use it (enough to not get you killed, but not enough to win a fight against someone who's been training with it, for example).
  • Mindsweep:
  • Able to read the minds of animals in the near vicinity by line of sight. If you can see them, you can consciously drop into the animal's mind and see what they see, hear what they hear, understand their thoughts, etc unless you're otherwise blocked by a stronger mental ability from others.
  • Resilience:
  • More resistant to elemental damage, increased temperature, poison attacks, etc. Able to withstand higher extremes than most other demigods, especially intense heat or cold due to their higher body temperature.

Freyja

General Ability - Sorceress
Easier to see through illusions, spells, mind tricks, etc. Can detect charms, curses, or other magical enhancements to objects such as weapons, clothing, or even physical locations. Harder to see through the Mist, but resistant to Mist effects such as brainwashing and other things.
Pick 1:

  • Words of Power:
  • A type of hypnotism or persuasion in which it allows the speaker to convince someone else to do or get whatever they want. The strength of the command depends on the tone and the emotion of the wielder's voice, as well as their skill with it.
  • Ferrokinesis:
  • Can summon and manipulate all non-magical/mythical gold. This includes changing the shape of it, summoning it from others, etc. If an item is summoned, said item must be at least half-gold for it to work - otherwise, only the gold will be extracted and summoned.
  • Call to Action:
  • Have the ability to manipulate emotions of love, fear, and war, including inspiring them in others or removing them in others. They can also heighten other emotions and sensations to a degree (provided there is prior out-of-character consent from the character affected).

Pick 2:

  • Alf Seidr:
  • Have the ability to heal either himself or others from wounds and injuries. This also comes with a glow that sustains from the wound while it heals as well as for a short time after. Larger injuries take more time and energy to heal and depletes more stamina than smaller injuries.
  • Telumkinesis:
  • Skilled with weapons; able to pick up any sword and use it well, and pick up any other weapon with a vague idea of how to use it (enough to not get you killed, but not enough to win a fight against someone who's been training with it, for example).
  • Valkyrie's Discretion:
  • Use a swan-feather cloak granted by Freyja herself to camouflage into one's surroundings. This ability can also shield and camouflage others, but only works on children of Freyja or Odin due to their ties to the Valkyries from their parents.
  • Luminescence:
  • Able to summon light and increase its brightness. Also resistant to bright lights or high luminosity environments (i.e. reflections off glass don't affect them as much). Cannot manipulate light beyond summoning it or changing brightness.
  • Cat-Kinesis:
  • Can speak to cats of any kind, including big cats. Recognized by cats regardless of claiming status as children of the deity. Prefers cats over dogs. Able to understand cats and to a lesser degree, see what the cats see and hear what they hear for a short amount of time (5 minutes).

Heimdallr

General Ability - Heightened Perception
Enhanced intelligence and knowledge. Never forget something they’ve seen before - photographic memory - but they have to have seen it beforehand.
Pick 1:

  • Light Bridge:
  • Able to teleport using the bifrost, stepping in and stepping out of it to get where they need to go. This often comes naturally once the child has been claimed or is aware of their power. They can also take others with them by holding them or keeping physical contact with them while traveling.
  • Watchman:
  • Have enhanced senses, reflexes, and instincts, particularly eyesight and hearing. This is an ability that they are naturally adapted to have. Children of this deity ‘see’ more than what meets the eye as well, and are naturally skilled at reading people and their emotions as well.
  • Breath of the Wild:
  • Possess a war horn that rallies nearby allies and nature itself to their aid. This horn is given to them by their father when claimed, and allows them to strengthen allies as well as call nature to help them (think like how in Narnia Aslan roaring makes everybody turn around and help, or Eren’s Titan scream…but for everything instead of just Titans).

Pick 2:

  • Shields:
  • Create shields out of light that radiate intense heat to those trying to break out. These shields are made out of the bifrost itself and are generally unbreakable without being dispelled, but they dispel automatically after 5 minutes or after the user is drained of energy/fallen unconscious.
  • Minor Photokinesis
  • Able to manipulate light - increase brightness, lower brightness, summon light, change the colors of something by light, etc. Unable to use light as a physical weapon or as a healing agent.
  • Truth-Teller:
  • Able to detect lies, whether spoken or by omission. If there are multiple lies, they will know which parts are the lies and which are the truth. They’re able to see illusions (although not seeing through them) and can see through the Mist easier than most other demigods and clear sighted mortals.
  • Radar
  • Can sense the life energy (or lack thereof) of demigods (only demigods) , animals, and nature alike just by being in close proximity (6-foot radius). Able to tell when someone is dying or when someone is born, by sensing their life energy, but cannot interfere with someone dying.
  • Maps
  • Strong internal compass. Able to tell distance and location very well, as well as read maps and navigate by the stars. Good with maps, location devices, compasses, etc, even in places with little to no landmarks.

Tyr

General Ability - Challenger
Naturally skilled in games of luck and chance, but physically unable to resist challenges. Often has increased dexterity in one or both hands.
Pick 1:

  • Unbreakable Vow:
  • Able to create binding contracts between two or more parties, like a weaker version of the Styx Swear. These contracts are near impossible to break, unless intentionally done, and the breaking of this contract will result in severe retribution for all parties involved, including the caster. Casters have the ability to annul this vow at any time with consent of all parties involved.
  • Retribution:
  • Have the ability to reflect any power or attack back on enemies - the larger the power/attack, the harder it is to reflect. If it's too big to reflect back safely, the power/attack will then be turned aside and rendered useless for a short time. Consecutive uses of this power become exponentially more draining.
  • Divine Order:
  • Restore tangibly 'broken' or damaged objects to their original state. This applies to physical wounds, shattered glass, dented shields, etc. You must be able to touch at least the surface in order to fix it, but once touched, all damaged sections will be repaired.

Pick 2:

  • Telumkinesis:
  • Skilled with weapons. Able to pick up any weapon in a pinch and use it well (not mastery) regardless of any prior training or lack thereof. Can place marks on weapons by touch to summon them at times of need. These marks are invisible to the naked eye but wear off after a few hours.
  • Sharpshot:
  • Proficiency with ranged weapons. Their shots will always fly true to their target provided they can clearly see their target, unless this ability is physically blocked (i.e. projectile blocked by an arrow/weapon interference).
  • Sacrificial:
  • See the risks and benefits of certain immediate choices (must happen in the same day/near future/next 24 hours) made by others. The more momentous the choice or decision, the harder it is to see clearly and discern the paths. Unable to interfere with the choice regardless of consequences, however - justice must be blind.
  • Speaker of Truth:
  • Can be a human lie detector - unable to directly lie themselves, but can conceal truth by omission. They can also tell when someone else is omitting the truth or lying, but cannot compel them to speak true.

Idunn

General Ability - Disney Princess
Understand (but not communicate with) animals. Generally knowledgeable about every living animal, mythical or non-mythical. Recognized by animals as a child of the deity regardless of claiming status, and animals are generally attracted to them.
Pick 1:

  • Golden Apple:
  • Enchant healing apples from regular apples by touch (think Minecraft ability, you touch it, it becomes magic and heals whoever eats it). Can only make about 2-3 a week due to the drainage of power and ability. These apples usually have healing abilities as well as energy boosting abilities and power buff abilities.
  • Hebekinesis:
  • Have the ability to manipulate the ages of themselves and others as well as control the consciousness of the person in their form (i.e. if you turn into a 4 year old, you can either shift your brain back to where it was when you were four, or keep your whatever age memories). Only one person can be age-changed at a time.
  • Spring Forward:
  • Able to manipulate time on a person, object, or event, up to 1 hour forward or 1 hour backwards. This lasts only for the hour before time resets back to its original time, or is dispelled by the user. Events may or may not play out the same way they did in the previous hour, and there will not be doubles of anyone - they will just be shoved backwards or forwards in time.

Pick 2:

  • New Beginnings:
  • Can ward off sickness, old age, and even death (if the person is not yet dead), while encouraging life, health, and personal growth just by their very presence. Unable to heal with a touch, and the 'healing aura' acts slower than other forms of magical healing, but it can be channeled to heal minor injuries.
  • Second Chance:
  • See the consequences and possible outcomes of a decision for 5 minutes and react accordingly. This includes all negative and positive outcomes and enables the wielder to pick the best outcome to achieve their chosen decision or path wished to be taken.
  • The Author:
  • Good at poetry, skilled at rhyme and other similar verbal abilities (you do not want to rap battle this person, for example). By verbal strength only, they can minorly curse or bless someone for a day (the extent would be 'not be able to walk in a straight line for 24 hours' or 'never lose a bet for the next 24 hours', etc).
  • Flower Power:
  • Cause plants to grow faster. Summon existing flowers and other vegetation out of the ground if they are there (i.e. you can't summon anything if the ground is frozen). Minor control of plants.
  • Stare:
  • "Control" animals by sheer intimidation (this only works on animals). Able to maintain order in difficult situations involving other creatures, but can only "control" animals until order is maintained.
